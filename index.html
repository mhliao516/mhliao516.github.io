Biography

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Cheng Long. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2018.

My general research interest lies in graph data management, particularly cohesive subgraph search and graph algorithms.
