Biography

I'm a PhD student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Ronghua Li. Previously, I received my bachelor degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2016.

My general research interest lies in spectral graph theory, which relates numerical linear algebra with combinatoric graph algorithms.
